Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

EcoGlow Candle Studio

The name reflects a handcrafted candle business focused on natural, eco‑friendly ingredients and warm ambiance. It is memorable, brand-friendly, and communicates the mission clearly.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase handcrafted candles, provide product recommendations, and offer information about custom orders. Visitors will be able to browse collections, learn about materials, and submit custom candle requests through an interactive form.

Scenarios

  • "What ingredients and fragrances are used in these candles?"
  • "How can I place a custom candle order for an event?"

Color Scheme

Selected colors:

  • #5A6A5A (Earthy Green) – Headings, accents
  • #F4EDE2 (Soft Cream) – Background, body sections

Typography

  • Montserrat – Headings
  • Roboto – Body text
